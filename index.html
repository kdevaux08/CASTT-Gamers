CASTT Gamers is a community build on its community members' passion for utilizing tabletop games, both RPGs and Board Games, in an applied and meaninful manner. We were founded in 2019 on the belief that we can support one each other and help each other strengthen our own use of tabletop games in our professional work. We strive to provide an inclusive and supportive community to allow everyone to practice, discuss, test, and play tabletop games. To join us, check our our Discord page, we welcome all professionals using tabletop games in an applied, spiritual, therapeutic manner.
